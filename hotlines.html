Hotlines

After Hour Emergencies:

My office is not an emergency facility and I do not provide 24 hour crisis or emergency therapy services. Should you experience an emergency or are in imminent danger to yourself or others, please call 911, or if you are able to safely transport yourself, go to the nearest emergency room.

Additional Resources and Crisis Lines:
  • National Suicide Hotline: (800) 273-8255
  • Texana Center 24 Hour Crisis Hotline (Fort Bend County Residents): (800) 633-5686
  • Fort Bend Crisis Intervention Team Non-Emergency Number: (281) 341-4665 and ask for CIT
  • DePelchin Children’s Center Mental Health Line: (713) 558-3777
  • The Harris Center 24 Hour Crisis Hotline (Harris County Residents): (713) 970-7000
