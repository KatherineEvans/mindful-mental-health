Welcome to Mindful Mental Health!

I believe therapy is for EVERYONE. You do not need to have a serious mental illness to participate in therapy. At times, we all struggle with change or experience challenges in life. Therapy offers a safe space to talk through those challenges, it allows you to explore your past and present emotions, thoughts, and experiences.

Therapy can help people:

  • Resolve conflicts
  • Relieve anxiety or stress
  • Cope with major life changes
  • Learn to manage unhealthy reactions
  • Create and maintain boundaries
  • Improve self-esteem
  • Change unhelpful/negative thoughts and beliefs
  • Improve coping skills
People are not cookie cutter and their treatment shouldn’t be either. Treatment should be individualized to meet the need of each individual person, which is why I take an integrative theoretical approach, incorporating several theories and techniques into treatment.
