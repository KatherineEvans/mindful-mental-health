Welcome to Mindful Mental Health!

Providing virtual therapy for Illinois and Texas residents

I believe that everyone deserves to feel heard, understood, and valued. My mission is to provide a safe, inclusive, and empowering environment where you can explore and grow. Together, we'll work towards your goals with compassion, understanding, mindfulness, and a focus on your unique strengths. Thank you for visiting, and I look forward to supporting you on your journey toward healing and growth.

Therapy can help people:

  1. Develop a deeper understanding of yourself
  2. Build self-esteem
  3. Foster resilience
  4. Improve your relationships with others
  5. Resolve conflicts
  6. Relieve anxiety or stress
  7. Create and maintain boundaries
  8. Cope with life transitions
  9. Change unhelpful/negative thoughts and beliefs
  10. Improve coping skills
  11. Cultivate present-moment awareness and acceptance
